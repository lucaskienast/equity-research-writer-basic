Example Co said FY25 revenue rose 6% YoY to $4.2bn, with adjusted EBIT up 11% to $620m as gross margin improved 80bps. Management said demand in North America remained soft in January and February, but pricing held up better than expected and cost actions supported profitability. Free cash flow was $410m versus $360m a year ago. Net debt / EBITDA fell to 1.4x from 1.9x.

The group said its Industrial segment grew 9% organically, Consumer declined 3%, and Asia Pacific grew 12% with strong orders in Japan and South Korea. Europe remained mixed.

Management reaffirmed FY26 guidance for mid-single-digit organic revenue growth and modest margin expansion, but said visibility remains limited in the first half and order intake is likely to remain uneven quarter to quarter.
